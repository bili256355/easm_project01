# V10.7_i append: pending tasks and forbidden interpretations

## Forbidden interpretations

Do not interpret V10.7_i as causal inference.

Do not require H_E2 to map to H_M. The whole reason for V10.7_i is that H may contribute to W45 through non-H M targets.

Do not use negative scalar mapping as evidence that H/WPSH has no W45 role. H may act through position, shape, ridge latitude, western extension, or object-relative geometry not captured by scalar strength proxies.

Do not control away P/V/Je/Jw as ordinary covariates. W45 is formed by these objects and should be treated as a configuration/reorganization problem.

- H should be treated as secondary in this scalar mapping only;
- do not generalize this to H/WPSH physical irrelevance.
